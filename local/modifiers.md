# Modifier Balance Sheet

## Difficulty Ratings
- Easy: +2 clicks, x1.5 score
- Medium: +4 clicks, x2.0 score  
- Hard: +6 clicks, x3.0 score

## Modifier List

### Easy Modifiers (+2 clicks)
| Name | Effect | Difficulty Factors |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Fog of War (Easy) | Only see 10 links | Requires more reading time |
| Scenic Route (Easy) | Min 8 clicks | Easy to achieve on most paths |
| Time Pressure (Easy) | 60 seconds | Generous time |

### Medium Modifiers (+4 clicks)
| Name | Effect | Difficulty Factors |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Fog of War (Medium) | Only see 6 links | Limited options |
| Don't Look Back | No revisiting | Requires planning |
| Button Smasher (Medium) | 2 clicks per move | Doubles click cost |
| Scenic Route (Medium) | Min 14 clicks | Forces longer paths |
| Time Pressure (Medium) | 50 seconds | Moderate pressure |

### Hard Modifiers (+6 clicks)
| Name | Effect | Difficulty Factors |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Fog of War (Hard) | Only see 3 links | Very limited choices |
| Button Smasher (Hard) | 4 clicks per move | Quadruples click cost |
| Detour | Visit specific type first | Path restriction |
| Time Pressure (Hard) | 35 seconds | High pressure |

## Modifier Synergies

### Dangerous Combinations
- ❌ Button Smasher + Scenic Route = Extreme click consumption
- ❌ Fog of War + Don't Look Back = Very difficult navigation
- ⚠️ Time Pressure + any modifier = High stress

### Manageable Combinations
- ✅ Fog of War + Scenic Route = Balanced challenge
- ✅ Don't Look Back + Time Pressure (Easy) = Skill test
- ✅ Detour + most modifiers = Planning challenge
