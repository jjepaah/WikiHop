# Shop Items Balance Sheet

## Permanent Upgrades (Active entire run)

| Item | Cost | Effect | Value Analysis |
|------|------|--------|----------------|
| Efficient Navigator | 15 | +2 clicks per stage | Breaks even after 7 stages |
| Master of Puppets | 18 | +2 clicks per modifier | Best with 2+ modifiers |
| Speed Reader | 12 | +15 sec to time modifiers | Situational (only with time mods) |
| Reading Glasses | 10 | Always see 5+ links | Counters Fog of War |

## Consumable Items (One-time use)

| Item | Cost | Effect | Best Used When |
|------|------|--------|----------------|
| Second Chance | 20 | Revive with 10 clicks | Late game safety net |
| Skip Target | 8 | New random target | Impossible target |
| Disable Modifier | 6 | Remove 1 modifier | Failed stage retry |
| 2 Free Rerolls | 5 | Shop rerolls (next shop) | Early game investment |

## Shop Pricing Philosophy
- **Early accessible** (5-12 clicks): Consumables, Reading Glasses
- **Mid-game investment** (15-18 clicks): Permanent upgrades
- **Late-game insurance** (20+ clicks): Second Chance

## Shop Appearance Rate
- Stage 1: After completion
- Stage 2-3: After every stage
- Stage 4+: After every stage (or every 2 stages to increase tension?)

## Reroll Costs
- First reroll: 4 clicks
- Second reroll: 6 clicks
- Third+ reroll: 8 clicks
